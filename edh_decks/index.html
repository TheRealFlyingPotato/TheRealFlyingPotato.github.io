Ghalta: Rawr


Smash into your opponents with Ghalta, Primal Hunger. Ramp quick into big beaters (Aggressive Mammoth, Forgotten Ancient, Ripjaw Raptor) to get him out and devastate with a 12/12 trampler. Has fetchlands because I run Panglacial Wurm because I think it's funny.

Kenrith: Big Boy Reanimator


Do you like playing big creatures but are tired of paying huge mana costs? Well here's a deck for you! Using Kenrith, the Returned King's last ability, reanimate massive beaters like Gisela, Blade of Goldnight, Aurelia, the Warleader, Etali, Primal Storm, Medomai the Ageless, Xenagos, God of Revels, and The Ur-Dragon and deal tons of damage!

Muldrotha: Sultai Graveyard Lands


Headed by Muldrotha, The Gravetide this deck plays a lot of lands and gets value from the graveyard. Tatyova, Benthic Druid, The Gitrog Monster both provide lots of value, and cards like Null Profusion and Recycle Give lots of card draw.

Ramos: 5-color retrace


While this jank deck is headed up by Ramos, Dragon Engine, the real commander of the deck is Wrenn and Six. Find him, Ult him, and have fun retracing a bunch of oddly fun spells. How do you win? I dunno! Copy some stuff, make some tokens, and see what happens! Dark Petition, Tamiyo, the Moon Sage, and Ral, Storm Conduit might give you a way another out though...

Tana Tymna: Token Beatdown Soul Sisters


Wreck face by pumping up Tana, the Bloodsower and beating face with her and a token army. Also has a lot of lifegain shenanigans for the fun of it. Have fun with stuff like Ajani, Caller of the Pride, Huatli, Radiant Champion, Rith, the Awakener and Power Matrix.
