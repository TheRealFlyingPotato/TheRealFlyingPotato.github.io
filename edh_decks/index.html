Chulane, Teller of Tales


A deck just thrown together from what I had lying around. Classic Chulane, Teller of Tales deck. Powerful because Chulane but not competitive by any means.

Feather, the Redeemed: Blink


With Feather, the Redeemed, abuse the reuse of Cloudshift effects to do a lot of stuff. Solemn Simulacrum, Mirror March, Molten Primordial, Chancellor of the Forge

Ghalta: Rawr


Smash into your opponents with Ghalta, Primal Hunger. Ramp quick into big beaters (Aggressive Mammoth, Forgotten Ancient, Ripjaw Raptor) to get him out and devastate with a 12/12 trampler. Has fetchlands because I run Panglacial Wurm because I think it's funny.

Kalamax: Shiny Spells and Voltron or Something


Built out of stuff I had lying around, Kalamax, the Stormsire draws a lot of cards and can do a lot of damage. Opportunity, Wavebreak Hippocamp, Fact or Fiction, Growth Spiral, Bounty of Might

Kami of the Crescent Moon


The second deck I ever made, Kami of the Crescent Moon is just a big group draw deck (Howling Mine, Font of Mythos) with a flicker package. Combo out with Peregrine Drake, Archaeomancer, and Ghostly Flicker.

Kels, Fight Fixer


My shiny pride and joy. Kels, Fight Fixer. Draw cards, get mana, play stuff, Zulaport Cutthroat everyone out. GG.

Kenrith: Big Boy Reanimator


Do you like playing big creatures but are tired of paying huge mana costs? Well here's a deck for you! Using Kenrith, the Returned King's last ability, reanimate massive beaters like Gisela, Blade of Goldnight, Aurelia, the Warleader, Etali, Primal Storm, Medomai the Ageless, Xenagos, God of Revels, and The Ur-Dragon and deal tons of damage!

Kenrith: Group Hug


You're everyone's friend with Kenrith, the Returned King group hug! All the mana doublers like Heartbeat of Spring, all the card draw in Howling Mine and Minds Aglow, and... yeah... you can definitely win with this deck, though it might be weird when you win with Happily Ever After, but hey! At least everyone will be happy with you. :)

Kenrith: I am a generous king


Kenrith, the Returned King: play Collective Voyage and Prosperity, take care of threats to you by giving gifts (Generous Gift, Settle the Wreckage) and copy spells. Has won with Happily Ever After.

Kenrith: Lifeline


Tutor out Lifeline, protect it with Sharuum the Hegemon, get loads of value with Court Hussar and Tishana, Voice of Thunder, and make loads of tokens with Elenda, the Dusk Rose.

Kenrith: Reanimator Combo


Combo deck that tries to win with Dockside Extortionist and a sac outlet or Necrotic Ooze combo lines. Psuedo cEDH.

Kenrith: Sagas


Reuse Sagas over and over with effects like Power Conduit and Ferropede.

Kenrith: Soul Sisters


With plenty of Soul Warden effects, this deck is based around lifegain triggers. Also has some neat engine pieces with +1/+1 counters if you get them together, such as Heliod, Sun-Crowned and Ghave, Guru of Spores. Drain with Epicure of Blood or beat face with Archangel of Thune, Krenko, Tin Street Kingpin, and Twinblade Paladin. Also plays Norin the Wary.

Kenrith: The Lich King


Lich and Kenrith, the Returned King seem like a good combo, no? Protect Lich with countermagic, hexproof, and play dumb draw cards like Ivory Tower and Gerrard, Capashen.

Kess, Dissident Mage


Built with stuff I had lying around, Kess, Dissident Mage plays a lot of spells. Steal stuff, either permanently or temporarily, and play all the nicol bolas planeswalkers.

Marwyn, the Nurturer


Marwyn, the Nurturer cEDH.

Muldrotha: Stax


Muldrotha, the Gravetide stax and hand hate. Psuedo cEDH.

Muldrotha: Sultai Graveyard Lands


Headed by Muldrotha, The Gravetide this deck plays a lot of lands and gets value from the graveyard. Tatyova, Benthic Druid, The Gitrog Monster both provide lots of value, and cards like Null Profusion and Recycle Give lots of card draw.

Muldrotha: Zombies


Play zombies. Muldrotha, the Gravetide at the helm.

Niv-Mizzet Reborn: Blink


With Niv-Mizzet Reborn at the helm, find Feather, the Redeemed or Izzet Chronarch and use Ghostly Flicker and cousins to do... a lot. Keruga, the Macrosage, Yorion, Sky Nomad, Purphoros, God of the Forge, Avenger of Zendikar

Ramos: 5-color retrace


While this jank deck is headed up by Ramos, Dragon Engine, the real commander of the deck is Wrenn and Six. Find him, Ult him, and have fun retracing a bunch of oddly fun spells. How do you win? I dunno! Copy some stuff, make some tokens, and see what happens! Dark Petition, Tamiyo, the Moon Sage, and Ral, Storm Conduit might give you a way another out though...

Rielle, the Everwise


Play all the bad wheels with Rielle, the Everwise. Read the Runes, Breakthrough, Tolarian Winds. Win with commander damage, Brallin, Skyshark Rider, or Psychosis Crawler

Sedris, the Traitor King: Unearthing the Dragons


Sedris, the Traitor King brings back big boys. Neheb, Dreadhorde Champion, Moraug, Fury of Akoum, Terror of the Peaks. Keep stuff around with Sundial of the Infinite, Thassa, Deep-Dwelling, Conjurer's Closet, and Teferi's Veil.

Sisay, Weatherlight Captain: Shrines / Upkeep Tribal


Get out Sanctum of All and have fun with way too many shrines. Also get out Paradox Haze, clone it, and have way too many upkeeps. Bringer of the Red Dawn, Outlaws' Merriment, Rite of the Raging Storm, Into the Wilds, Stolen Strategy, As Foretold, Junkyo Bell, Gate to the Aether

Tana Tymna: Token Beatdown Soul Sisters


Wreck face by pumping up Tana, the Bloodsower and beating face with her and a token army. Also has a lot of lifegain shenanigans for the fun of it. Have fun with stuff like Ajani, Caller of the Pride, Huatli, Radiant Champion, Rith, the Awakener and Power Matrix.

Tasigur: Seasons Pastigur


Tasigur, the Golden Fang cEDH.

Tormod + Sidar: Desecrated Existence


With Sidar, Kondo of Jamuraa and Tormod, the Desecrator at the helm, get out Tortured Existence and go to town. Desecrated Tomb, Golgari Brownscale, Nature's Will

Yore-Tiller Nephilim


With the unofficial Yore-Tiller Nephilim at the helm, bring back massive beaters and value cards. Dream Eater, Divinity of Pride, Arbiter of the Ideal, Gyruda, Doom of Depths, Athreos, Shroud-Veiled, Reya Dawnbringer
