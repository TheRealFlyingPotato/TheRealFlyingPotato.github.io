Master of the Ancient Magic SETUP Deal 2 Minor Powers face-up as your initial Ancient Magic cards. SPECIAL RULES Arcane Watcher You may play your Ancient Magic cards as though they were in your hand. Doing so costs 1 energy less and does not provide any elements. Powers played this way are not dicarded at end of turn and instead are kept as your Ancient Magic cards. Whenever you have more than 2 Ancient Magic Power cards, forget one. If a {fast} power is ever one of your Ancient Magic cards, replace it with the top power of the Minor power deck. (This repeats as neccessary.) Offer Guidance to Other Spirits When another spirit gains a power, they may instead (with your permission) gain one of your Ancient Magic cards. If they do, gain a Minor Power to your Ancient Magic cards. Replace 1 {explorer} with 1 {dahan} Replace 1 {town} with 1 {dahan} Pay 3 energy to repeat this power in an adjacent land, even if it has no {dahan}. Until time passes, you do not forget powers from your Ancient Magics when you have more than 2. Gain a Minor Power to your Ancient Magics. Target spirit gains a Minor Power.
