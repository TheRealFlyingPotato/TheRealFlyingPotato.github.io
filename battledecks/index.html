Conduit Sagas

This deck focuses on using Power Conduit along with Chainer's Torment to repeatedly drain life from your opponents. The Eldest Reborn is another way for Power Conduit to be abused, while cards such as Ill-Gotten Inheritance, Defiant Bloodlord, and Agent of Masks assist in the draining.

Gates

A 5-color battle deck? Yes, in fact! This deck uses 22 guildgates (such as Azorius Guildgate) and takes advantage of them with Gatebreaker Ram, Gate Colossus, Guild Summit, and Archway Angel.
